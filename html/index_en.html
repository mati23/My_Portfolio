Sobre Mim

Computer Engineer with training completed in the first half of 2018. Since childhood stimulating my creative side and always trying to innovate how people see my work, and could not be different when I discovered software development, in which motivation and commitment make me transform lots of (very much) caffeine in code.

I worked as an apprentice on the scholarship of the Cooperative Learning Program in Student Cells (PACCE) where I had my first contact with Arduino and Android development. Then I was a fellow in the Nucleus of Technological Development (NuDeTec) performing 3D simulations using the Blender 3D tool. And finally, I joined the junior company Loading Development Junior where I started to have professional experience in the field of Marketing and Development until the end of my academic journey.

At the end of the course I developed a project for an Android 3D reconstruction application and I contacted IoT (Internet of Things), in which I was able to develop, together with the Loading Jr. team, an electronic check-in system using RFID technologies (Radio Frequency Identification).
